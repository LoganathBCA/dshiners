⚡ Featured Case Study · E-Commerce

Kadhambam Dry Fruits & Nuts
E-Commerce Platform · React · QR Payments

Custom React E-Commerce Zero-Cost Backend Architecture QR Payment Integration Mobile-First UI Serverless Data System
🔴 The Business Challenge

High Costs. Zero Tolerance for Them.

Kadhambam needed a fully functional e-commerce storefront — complete with a live admin panel for managing customized products and categories — but with a non-negotiable constraint: zero recurring monthly costs for backend server hosting. On top of that, traditional payment gateways come loaded with setup overhead and per-transaction fees that cut directly into a retail brand's margins. The brief was unambiguous: deliver enterprise-grade shopping infrastructure on a lean, cost-zero operational model.

🟢 The Value Engineering

Three Precise Solutions. Zero Compromise.

  • 🎨
    Abudhaheer mapped a mobile-first shopping interface that mirrors exactly how local shoppers browse — fast category filtering, clean product displays, and a friction-free checkout journey optimized for speed and conversion on any device.
  • ⚙️
    Infant Raj engineered a React-powered e-commerce engine that handles live product and category updates entirely client-side — eliminating any dependency on paid backend server infrastructure and driving monthly operational costs to absolute zero.
  • 💳
    The team replaced the traditional payment gateway — with its compliance overhead and per-transaction fee cuts — with a secure, streamlined UPI QR code payment flow. Customers pay instantly from any app. The brand keeps every rupee of every sale.
Result: 100% mobile-responsive e-commerce platform with zero backend hosting costs and zero payment gateway transaction fees.
